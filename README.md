# WITS Old Mutual Insure Risk Lab

A partnership between the University of the Witwatersrand and Old Mutual Insure tackling climate risk, infectious disease modelling, and sustainable insurance through advanced statistical and machine learning methods.

## Website

Visit the live site: [https://odeymofokeng-wits.github.io/wits-risk-lab/](https://odeymofokeng-wits.github.io/wits-risk-lab/)

## About

The WITS Old Mutual Insure Risk Lab is a research laboratory based in the School of Statistics and Actuarial Science at the University of the Witwatersrand. We bring together researchers, lecturers, and students working on quantitative risk assessment across African contexts.

## Research Areas

- Climate Risk Modelling
- Machine Learning & AI
- Health & Survival Analysis
- Spatial Statistics
- Data Visualisation
- Risk & Insurance Analytics

## Team

Our team includes specialists in Bayesian methods, spatial statistics, survival analysis, multivariate analysis, and actuarial science.
